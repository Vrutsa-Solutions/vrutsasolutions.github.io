Countries we specialise in

Deep expertise across the most popular and emerging study destinations for Indian students.

🇺🇸 United States

  • F-1 visa, OPT, STEM extension, and H-1B pathway planning
  • Top universities across CS, business, engineering, and healthcare
  • GRE/GMAT and TOEFL prep integrated into your timeline
  • On-campus work authorisation and CPT guidance

🇬🇧 United Kingdom

  • Tier 4 Student Visa and Graduate Route (2-year PSW)
  • Russell Group universities and specialised postgrad programs
  • Chevening, Commonwealth, and university scholarships
  • NHS surcharge, BRP card, and banking setup guidance

🇨🇦 Canada

  • Study Permit, Co-op work permits, and PGWP (3 years)
  • Province-specific PR pathways — Ontario, BC, Alberta
  • IELTS requirements and SPP-eligible institutions
  • Cost-effective universities with strong ROI for Indian students

🇦🇺 Australia & 🇩🇪 Germany

  • Australia: Student visa subclass 500, Skilled Migration, GSM
  • Germany: free/low tuition public universities, APS process
  • Both: strong post-study work rights and PR pathways
  • Language preparation — IELTS (AUS) and German A2+ (DEU)
