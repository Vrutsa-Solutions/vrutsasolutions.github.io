Our Market Entry Playbook — 5 phases

From research to revenue — a disciplined entry process that avoids the expensive surprises most companies encounter.

1

Market Screening & Selection

Evaluate 3–5 potential markets against your product-market fit criteria, competitive density, regulatory complexity, and addressable opportunity. Select with data, not intuition.

2

Deep Market Intelligence

On-the-ground customer interviews, distributor conversations, regulatory mapping, and competitor forensics. Know the market from the inside before you enter.

3

Entry Strategy & Business Case

Choose the right entry mode — organic build, JV, partnership, acquisition, or digital-first — with a full financial model and 24-month P&L projection for board sign-off.

4

Setup & Launch

Entity setup, team hire or partner activation, systems deployed, and go-to-market executed. Vrutsa is on the ground with you for the first 90 days to course-correct in real time.

5

Scale & Localise

Once early revenue is validated, Vrutsa builds the local team capability, localised product features, and regional supply chain needed to compete as a native player — not a foreign entrant.
